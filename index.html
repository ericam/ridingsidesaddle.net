Sam gasps for air and finds herself alone. Herman gasps for air and finds herself alone. They wipe the mud from their body.

Riding SideSaddle* is an open-source novel on notecards — a fragmented memory of our time together, and these bodies we inhabit — created by Eric Suzanne, with contributions from Jacob Liechty, Sondra Eby, Aaron Liechty, and others. Published by SpringGun Press, and made available under the Creative Commons Attribution-NonCommercial-ShareAlike 4.0 International License .

Riding SideSaddle* was written using the OddBooks editor, and is available online with aditional multimedia features (still in development), including links, tags, animations, music, and tools for open-source collaboration.
