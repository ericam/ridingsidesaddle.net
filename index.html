Rarely do you get to experience intimate thoughts like these. It's like being dropped into someone's head, Quantum Leap style, in the middle of a transformative time in their life. I won't deny, it definitely turned me on.
Riding SideSaddle* is an open-source novel on note cards, created by Eric M Suzanne, with contributions from Jacob Liechty, Sondra Eby, Aaron Liechty, and others.
